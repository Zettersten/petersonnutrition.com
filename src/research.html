Peterson Nutrition and Fitness: Services

Specialties: Research

Bringing you tomorrow medicines today… Commonwealth Clinical Research Specialist (CCRS) has been conducting clinical research trials since 1998. Elisabeth currently contracts with CCRS where she provides nutritional counseling for individuals involved with specific studies researching new medicines.

Elisabeth meets with individuals per protocol requirements, generally monthly for a year or two, to educate and counsel individuals regarding nutrition and activity.

CCRS offers individuals:

  • Access to cutting edge medicines while still in a clinical trial at no cost.
  • Personal attention and professional care at no cost.
  • Opportunities to learn more abut their condition and overall health.
  • Flexible hours.
  • Often compensation for time and travel.

For more information on research studies or how to get involved contact Commonwealth Clinical Research Specialist at http://ccrsva.com or 804-288-7425.
