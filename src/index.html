PETERSON NUTRITION & FITNESS, RICHMOND, VA:

Nutrition and fitness guidance for individuals and groups

Peterson Nutrition & Fitness of Richmond, VA was founded in 2001 and is the private practice of Elisabeth Peterson, RD, CEDRD. Peterson Nutrition & Fitness exists to educate and empower each individual to effect lifestyle changes toward optimal wellness through balance, comprehension and application of the complex nutritional and activity requirements of the body.

Grounded in current dietetic science and research, Elisabeth uses the somato-psycho-social model and incorporates non-diet concepts, mindful eating, body acceptance, and the use of story and metaphor with individuals, families and couples to facilitate client progress towards normalized eating internationally for over 25 years.

Elisabeth co-founded Light of the Moon Café. She has facilitated Eating in the Light of the Moon groups and Wise Women groups based on Dr. Anita Johnston’s work in Richmond, Virginia for over 10 years. Elisabeth, an iaedp certified eating disorder registered dietitian, supervises RD’s internationally for iaedp certification and is on the Certification Committee for iaedp.
