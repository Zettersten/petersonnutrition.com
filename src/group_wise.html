Group Counseling

The Wise Women’s Group

The Wise Women’s Group provides a sanctuary for each woman to use her own voice, speak from her heart, share successes and struggles, nurture & trust her own intuition, practice using new recovery skills, and provide a witness for each others therapeutic work. Many of the women who attend Wise Women have completed the Eating in the Light of the Moon Group, but that is not a requirement.

Wise Women sit in circle twice a month for two hours. The cost is $55/group. If you feel that the Wise Women Group would support your recovery from eating difficulties, please contact me.
