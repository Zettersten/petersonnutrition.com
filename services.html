Peterson Nutrition and Fitness: Services

Individual and group nutrition and fitness programs

At the foundation of Peterson Nutrition & Fitness is an ardent desire to empower clients by assuring that each individual gains a comfortable and employable understanding of the complex nutritional and activity tools needed to achieve optimal health. Compassion, empathy and attention to individual needs have yielded a high success rate of goal achievement for my clientele.

Elisabeth’s counseling addresses the nutritional and general wellness needs of people of all ages. She specializes in the therapeutic support for those suffering from all types of eating disorders and also provides instruction in sports nutrition, vegetarian nutrition, high risk pregnancy, reducing health risks and increasing quality of life, and disease states including diabetes, hypertension, cardiovascular disease, food allergies, and celiac sprue.

Elisabeth strives to support an inclusive environment respectful to all people regardless of race, ethnicity, religion, age, veteran status, ability, immigration or economic status, gender or sexual orientation.

IN PERSON, PHONE, OR TELEHEALTH SESSIONS. ONLINE CLASS/GROUP AVAILABLE TOO.
